Short Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor at Northeast Foreastry University with award of the Chengdong Outstanding Youths. My research interests include advanced robots (and aircraft) design and control, learning-based approaches, image processing, and their applications to Foreastry/Agriculture Tasks.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Aeronautical and Astronautical Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2023, supervised by Prof. Lixian Zhang. I was a visiting researcher at Faculty of Aerospace Engineering, Delft University of Technology (TU-Delft), the Netherlands, co-supervised by Dr. Sherry Wang. Prior to PhD, I received a B.Eng. degree in Automation from Northeast Foreastry University (NEFU) and a MS.c. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Harbin, China, in 2017 and 2019, respectively.

Fields of Interests

Flight control, Switched Systems, Deep learning, Image Processing, Specialized robots, and their applications to foreastry/agriculture Tasks.
